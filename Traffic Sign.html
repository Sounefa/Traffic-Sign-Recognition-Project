Project Overview

This project enhances self-driving safety by enabling autonomous cars to recognize road traffic signs using deep learning. Built on the German Traffic Sign Dataset (GTSDB/GTSRB), the model uses a custom AlexNet CNN to classify real-world traffic signs under varied conditions such as lighting, angles, occlusions, and motion.
